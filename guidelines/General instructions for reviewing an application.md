# General instructions for reviewing an application

Access the reviewer form found here: [Accr. Templates - Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/placeholder_accr_templates)

Copy the template for your candidate with their name and your name/initials in their folder. A.Stat. application folders are found here: [2. Applicants - Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/placeholder_astat_applicants) and P.Stat. applications here: [2. Applicants - Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/placeholder_pstat_applicants)

Use their submitted materials and the accreditation guides that provide information on what is required to assess your candidates. The supporting guides can be found here: [Instructions for Completing the Application Form for A.Stat. (Associate Statistician) | Statistical Society of Canada (ssc.ca)](https://ssc.ca/en/accreditation/instructions-completing-application-form-astat-associate-statistician)

And very importantly here: [accreditation-document-accreditation-ana2020](https://ssc.ca/sites/default/files/imce/accreditation-document-accreditation-ana2020_-_google_docs.pdf).

Depending on the university, you may also be able to assess their courses as appropriate by reviewing the accredited institutions and their course lists. They can found here: [Accredited Courses | Statistical Society of Canada (ssc.ca)](https://ssc.ca/en/accredited-courses)

****It’s important to note that A.Stat. applicants only need to meet the educational criteria. No work experience is required!*****
